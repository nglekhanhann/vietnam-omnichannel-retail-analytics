# Dashboard page guide

The Power BI report uses a 16:9 canvas and contains six report pages.

## 1. Home

Landing page with project scope, report navigation, and shortcuts to each analytical section.

## 2. Retail Sales Overview

Summarizes overall business performance through headline KPIs and comparison visuals. The page is designed to answer whether sales volume, net sales, gross profit, and gross margin are moving in a healthy direction.

## 3. Sales & Profit Trends

Focuses on monthly and yearly movement in net sales, gross profit, gross margin, discount rate, and growth. It highlights seasonality and periods where high revenue may be driven by deeper discounts.

## 4. Channel & Store Analysis

Compares Offline Store, Shopee, Mobile App, Website, Lazada, TikTok Shop, and Facebook Shop. It also evaluates store, city, region, and store-format performance.

## 5. Item Sales

Analyzes category, sub-category, SKU, revenue contribution, margin quality, and ABC product classification. It supports product-priority and assortment decisions.

## 6. Promotion & Risk Analysis

Evaluates promotion-led sales quality, return patterns, and stockout risk. Risk analysis is considered at channel × category level rather than only through overall averages.
